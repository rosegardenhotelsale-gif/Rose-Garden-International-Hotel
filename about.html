Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence Since 2010

Founded in 2010 in the vibrant heart of Phnom Penh, Rose Garden International Hotel has grown from a visionary concept into a landmark destination. We set out with a simple yet ambitious goal: to create a sanctuary of luxury that honors the rich cultural heritage of Cambodia while offering world-class amenities to our discerning guests.

Over the years, we have remained deeply committed to our core philosophy of blending authentic Cambodian hospitality with international luxury standards. Every detail of our hotel, from the architectural design to our personalized guest services, has been carefully curated to provide an unforgettable experience of elegance, comfort, and warmth.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

We are committed to providing exceptional hospitality experiences by anticipating our guests' needs, delivering personalized services, and creating a welcoming atmosphere where every stay is memorable.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the leading luxury hotel in Southeast Asia, renowned for our unparalleled service quality, innovative guest experiences, and deep-rooted respect for local culture and community.

Our Values

We operate with unwavering integrity, strive for excellence in every detail, embody the genuine warmth of Cambodian hospitality, and passionately champion sustainability in all our practices.
