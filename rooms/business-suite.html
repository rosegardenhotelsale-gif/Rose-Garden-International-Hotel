A Perfect Retreat for Every Traveler

The Business Suite at Rose Garden International Hotel is the ultimate sanctuary for discerning business travelers and those who demand the finest in both work and relaxation. Encompassing an impressive 65m², this expansive suite features a luxurious king-size bed in a serene private bedroom, complemented by a separate living area that seamlessly transitions into a fully equipped executive workspace. Panoramic floor-to-ceiling windows offer sweeping views of the city, creating an inspiring backdrop for both productive days and restful evenings.

The suite's interior reflects a harmonious blend of contemporary elegance and functional sophistication. The dedicated work zone features an oversized executive desk, ergonomic chair, high-speed wired internet, and a multi-function printer — everything needed for seamless business operations. The living area includes a plush sofa, accent seating, a dining table for private meetings or in-room dining, and a premium entertainment system with a 55-inch smart TV and surround sound speakers.

The spa-inspired marble bathroom boasts a walk-in rain shower, a standalone soaking tub, dual vanities, and a curated selection of luxury bath products. Exclusive suite amenities include a premium Nespresso machine, a fully stocked premium minibar, complimentary pressing service, and access to the Executive Lounge with complimentary refreshments. With dedicated butler-style service, express check-in/out, and 24-hour concierge assistance, the Business Suite ensures that every aspect of your stay is handled with the highest level of care.
