A Perfect Retreat for Every Traveler

The Deluxe Twin Room at Rose Garden International Hotel combines the spacious elegance of our Deluxe category with the flexibility of twin sleeping arrangements. Spanning 42m² of refined living space, this room features two plush twin beds outfitted with premium Egyptian cotton linens, offering the perfect accommodation for friends, colleagues, or family members who prefer separate beds without compromising on luxury. Floor-to-ceiling windows reveal panoramic views of Phnom Penh's captivating cityscape.

Designed with meticulous attention to detail, the Deluxe Twin Room showcases warm wood accents, rich textures, and sophisticated lighting that create an inviting yet refined atmosphere. The expansive marble bathroom features both a rain shower and a deep soaking bathtub, premium bath amenities, heated towel rails, and illuminated vanity mirrors — providing a spa-inspired retreat within the comfort of your room.

Modern conveniences abound with complimentary high-speed WiFi, a 50-inch smart TV with premium streaming services, Bluetooth speakers, and a Nespresso coffee machine. The dedicated executive work desk ensures productivity when needed, while 24-hour room service, twice-daily housekeeping, and personalized turndown service guarantee that every moment of your stay is effortlessly comfortable and memorable.
