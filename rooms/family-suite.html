A Perfect Retreat for Every Traveler

Welcome to the Family Suite at Rose Garden International Hotel — a spacious 85m² haven designed with families in mind. This beautifully appointed suite features a private master bedroom with a luxurious king-size bed, plus a separate children's room with two comfortable twin beds, ensuring that every family member enjoys their own restful retreat. Large windows throughout the suite frame tranquil garden views, creating a peaceful oasis amidst the vibrant energy of Phnom Penh.

The suite's thoughtful layout includes a generous living area where the family can gather to relax, play, or plan the day's adventures. A cozy dining nook is perfect for shared meals ordered from our extensive room service menu. The design blends playful warmth with elegant sophistication — soft colors, natural textures, and child-friendly furnishings create a welcoming environment for guests of all ages. The main bathroom features a walk-in rain shower and a deep bathtub perfect for little ones, while a second guest bathroom adds convenience for larger families.

Every detail has been considered for the comfort and safety of your family. In-room amenities include complimentary high-speed WiFi, two smart TVs (50-inch in the master and 43-inch in the children's room), a Nespresso machine, a well-stocked minibar with complimentary soft drinks and snacks for children, a full-size in-room safe, and a baby cot or extra bed available upon request. With twice-daily housekeeping, 24-hour room service, a dedicated family concierge, and complimentary access to the kids' club, the Family Suite promises a truly memorable stay for the whole family.
